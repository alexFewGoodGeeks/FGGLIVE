Design work for Aalto University

  • Department of Bioproducts and Biosystems - School of Chemical Technology

    Illustrations of the various stages of AFM (Atomic Force Microscope) interacting with integrins on cell surface. All illustrations have been used as a visual representation of the concepts defined as part of the published scientific paper.

  • Illustration of cell colony that has active and inactive integrins accompanied by illustration of those integrins on a cell membrane.

  • Illustration of AFM tip (atomic force microscope) interacting with integrin rich and integrin poor areas on cell colony.

  • Illustration of integrins (active and inactive) interacting with cellulose nanofibrils (left) and laminin (middle and right).
