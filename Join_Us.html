Senior Front-End Developer in ReactJS

For a Swiss Client of ours, Few Good Geeks Oy is looking for a Senior Front-End Developer in ReactJS with 3 - 5 Years of experience in a relevant position. We need candidates driven by curiosity and willingness to grow in this broad function.

About the client

The client is fundamentally changing the way in which people use their cars. The client is Switzerland’s greatest car sharing platform that allows private individuals and companies to easily and safely share their vehicles with others. The client is firmly located at the interface of two megatrends: Collaborative Economy and Mobility of the Future. They are a joint venture enterprise involving Migros/m-way, Mobiliar insurances, Amag and Mobility Carsharing.

The Job

You enjoy new challenges and never lose sight of the overall picture in hectic situations. You work using the agile SCRUM process. Ideally, you already have start-up experience. You are comfortable with assuming responsibility and are not afraid to make mistakes. You will work directly with our Senior Lead Developer, our Frontend Developer and the Product Owner.

Responsibilities:

• On-going development of the Frontend based on React;
• Innovation/contributing to shaping new functionalities/optimization;
• Process optimization within all systems;
• Feasibility analysis of new concepts;
• Collaboration with external resources.


Job requirements:

• Proven professional experience in software development using Ruby on Rails, along with other common libraries;
• A knack for writing clean, readable ES6 code;
• Solid understanding of object-oriented programming;
• Experience in the sectors of software architecture/systems planning and systems security;
• Proven professional experience with the SCRUM process;
• High levels of initiative, self-motivation and dedication;
• English is your working language; knowledge of German beneficial.


Moreover, you have experience with:

• Ruby on Rails knowledge is a strong plus;
• RESTful APIs and web services;
• Ajax and JSON;
• Relational databases and SQL;
• Deep HTML5, CSS3 knowledge;
• MVC, Stateless Services, SOLID principles;
• TDD, Unit Testing and continuous integration;
• Version Control, in particular Git;
• UNIX/Linux/OSX experience;
• Experience with Node.js and Docker ecosystem will be considered a plus.
